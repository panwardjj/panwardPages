My First Time Hiking

Location: Phu Kradueng

Duration Time: 4-7 Jan 2024

I just had the opportunity to go hiking for the first time at Phu Kradueng earlier this year. It was a trip that I was very excited and happy about. The top of the mountain has beautiful views and cool weather. Being surrounded by nature makes me feel at ease. In the end, I'm very glad that I was able to try this adventure and have a good experience. If I have a chance, I will definitely go back.

How to travel?

Take a bus from Mo Chit to Pha Nok Khao in Loei Province, then transfer to a minibus to Phu Kradueng National Park.

What activities?

Biking, Long journey, Watch the sunrise/sunset
